## 1.1 bilateral vs.unilateral contract

- bilateral contract:a contract formed by an ***exchange of promises***
- unilateral contract:involves only 1 promise(by offeror)that expressly require ***performance to accept***.contract in formed ***only when*** offeree completes performance.

## 1.2 express vs.implied contract

- express:formed by express written or oral terms.
- implied-in-fact:formed by ***conduct*** of the parties.
- implied-in-law/Quasi-contract:***not*** a contract,is a ***restitution remedy*** to prevent unjust enrichment.

## 1.3 void,voidable,& unenforceable

- void:no conract exists,agreemnet has ***no legal effect***.
- voidable:problem with the contract exists,but it ***may*** be enforced or rejected at the ***opion*** of one party.
- unenforceable:is a valid contract that ***cannot*** be enforced cuz a defense applies.

# 2.applicable law

## 2.1[H]UCC & CL

- UCC Art.2:governs ***sale of goods*** contracts.
- CL:applies to ***all other*** contracts(e.g.sevices,construction,real properly).
- Mixed contracts:***predominant purpose*** of the contract controls the law to apply.

## 2.2[H] UCC Art.2

- goods:all things moveable at the time of identification to the contract(ex:money/currency).

## 2.3 element to form a contract

- mutual assent（offer + acceptance）
- consideration or valid substitue
- no defense

# 3.[H]mutual assent

## 3.1 offer

- a ***manifestation of present intent*** to enter into a contract.
- with ***definite & reasonably certain terms***
- ***communicated*** to an identifited offeree.

### 3.1.1 termination

> an offer can be terminated ***before acceptance*** by:

- ***revocation*** by offeror.[要约人直接向受要约人传达撤销/受要约人从可靠来源获悉了合理表明要约已被撤销的信息（例如，房屋已售予另一位买家）.
- ***rejection*** or counteroffer by offeree.
- ***lapse of time***:time for acceptance expires after(a)time limit stated,or (b)reasonable time(if no time stated);
- ***death or incapacity*** of either party;
- ***supervening illegaility***-proposed contract becomes illegal ***after*** offer is made.

### 3.1.2 revocation

> an offer may be revoked at any time before acceptance by:

- ***unambiguous words or conduct*** indicating an unwillingness/inability to contract ***communicated to offeree***;
- ***indirect revocation***-when (1)offeror takes ***definite action inconsistent*** with entering into a proposed contract,and (2)offeree  acquires reliable info to that effect.

### 3.1.3 irrevocable offers[不可撤回的要约]

- ***option contract(CL)***:a promise to keep an offer open supported by consideration.[视为一个单独的选择权合同]
- ***UCC merchant's firm offer***:requires(1)an offer by a merchant's to buy/sell goods[提出人必须是商家],(2)in a signed writing[书面提出],(3)stating offer will be help open(irrevocability ***CANNOT*** exceed 3 months[即使要约中声明了更长的期限],(4)separately signed by offeror(if form was supplied by offeree).(5)consideration is ***NOT*** required.
- ***detrimental reliance/promissory estoppel***:offer was reasonably relied on to offeree's detriment.[要约人可合理预见到受要约人将信赖其要约 + 受要约人已合理信赖该要约并因此遭受损害]
- ***start performance on unliateral contract***:makes offer temporarily irrevocable for a reasonable time to complete performance.[要约人仅以履行方式邀请承诺，且受要约人已开始履行,mere preparation is insufficient].

### 3.1.4 rejection

- ***counteroffer***:is both a rejection & new offer.[要约人直接向受要约人传达/受要约人从可靠来源获悉了合理表明要约已被撤销的信息。例如，房屋已售予另一位买家].
- ***conditional acceptance***:treated same as counteroffer.
- ***acceptance with varying terms***:it's either acceptance or a counteroffer.

## 3.2 acceptance

### 3.2.1 by words/conduct

- express words or conduct manifesting acceptance.

### 3.2.2 by performance

- ***bilateral contracts***:start of performance manifests acceptance.
- ***unilateral contracts***:only makes the offer irrevocable (acceptance only when completed).

### 3.2.3 by silence

- ***NOT*** acceptance,unless an implied-in-Fact contract.

### 3.2.4 by shipment of goods

- is acceptance if (a)promise to ship goods,or (b)prompt shipment of goods(unless it's an accommodation shipment).

## 3.3 【M】acceptance with varying/additional terms

### 3.3.1 mirror image rule (CL)

- acceptance ***MUST*** exactly mirror the offer,any variations constitute a counteroffer.
- A purported acceptance that contains different or additional terms acts as a rejection of the original offer and as a counteroffer(均构成对原要约的拒绝和一项反要约).

### 3.3.2 UCC

> acceptance DOES NOT have to mirror the offer to form a contract.BUT,***additional or different terms*** are incl only if:

- both parties r merchants;
- the term is ***not*** a material change;
- offer ***does not*** expressly limit acceptance to the exact offer;
- no objection was made with a reasonable time.
- ***material change***:likely to cause hardship/surprise(e.g.disclaimer of warrantly,arbitration clause).

## 3.4 [H] timing of offer/acceptance & mailbox rule

### 3.4.1 offer

- effective when ***received***.

### 3.4.2 acceptance

- effective when ***dispatched***.[承诺通常在发出时生效]
- ***EXP***:acceptance of an option contract is effective upon receipt.

### 3.4.3 revocation or rejection/counteroffer

- effective when ***received***.[拒绝则在收到时生效]

### 3.4.4 mailbox rule[邮箱规则]

- 先寄同意，后寄拒绝，则前一个寄出时合同已经成立。***EXP*** 要约人先收到了拒绝+基于信赖作出安排。
- 要约人提出撤回，对方知悉前寄出了同意，合同依然成立。
- 先寄拒绝，后寄接受，以对方 ***先收到*** 的为准，***不适用*** 邮箱规则。
- 为承诺一项要约，受要约人必须表现出受要约条款约束的客观意图 + 遵守要约中关于承诺的时间、地点或方式的任何具体规定，此时 ***不适用*** 邮箱规则。e.g.要约声明，承诺必须在6月25日之前被收到才有效，实际上在26号收到，此时该承诺是无效的。

## 3.5 [M]implied-in-Fact contract

> treated the same as an express contract,a contract is created by conduct if:

- the conduct is intentional,AND
- each party has reason to know the other party will interpret the conduct as an agreement.

## 3.6 [M]indefiniteness/absence of terms

> if terms of an agreement are ***not cetain***(can't be ascertained to a reasonable degree of certainty),then it's NOT enforceable.

- 4 terms r required:parties + subject matter + price + time for performance.
- UCC:only essential term is ***quantity***.
- ***indefinite duration***:contract is generally invalid.
- ***real property***:requires sufficient ***description of the land*** + price.

# 4.[H]consideration

## 4.1 not sufficient

- ***past/moral consideration***
- ***promise of gift***
- ***sham[虚假]/nominal/token[象征性] consideration***
- ***illusory promise***

## 4.2 consideration substitutes/excp

## 4.2.1 material benefit rule

- a promise made in recognition of a ***benefit previously received*** is binding to the extent necessary to ***prevent injustice***.
- ***BUT***,not applied when:(a) conferred as a gift,or (b)value of promise is ***disproportional*** to the benefit conferred.

## 4.2.2 promissory estoppel[承诺性禁反言]/detrimental reliance

- applies when (1)a party reasonably & foreseeably relied to their detriment on other party's promise,(2)promisor reasonably expected a change of position in reliance,(3)it's necessary to avoid injustice.
- ***promise to pay a non-legally enforceable past debt***,is binding without new consideration if in ***writing***.
- 不要求证明受诺人的信赖为允诺人带来了经济利益。
- 不要求所承诺的赠与价值必须与受诺人因信赖而遭受的损失大致相当。但是，受诺人的救济仅限于因信赖承诺而遭受的实际损失——而不必然是承诺的全部价值。

## 4.3[H]modification of contracts

### 4.3.1 pre-existing duty rule(CL)

#### (1)general

- past performance or a pre-existing duty is NOT adequate consideration.

#### (2)EXP

- ***addition or change*** in performance.
- ***unforeseen circumstances***:a fair and equitable modification due to severe unanticipated circumstances AND contract isn't fully performed by either party;
- ***third-party promise***:where duty was owed to a third-person(not promisor).

### 4.3.2 UCC

- NO consideration is required for contract modifications made in good faith.[只要求双方协议+善意,ie,诚实并遵守合理的商业公平交易标准]
- BUT,must have a writing if:(a)it falls within the SOF,(b)original contract states that modification must be in writing.

# 5.defenses to formation/enforceablility

## 5.1 [M] incapacity[contract voidable]

### 5.1.1 lack of meantal capacity

- can't understand the contract's meaning & effect
- ***intoxication***:a lack of capacity ***only if*** other person has reason to know of intoxication.

### 5.1.2 minors-under 18yrs

#### (1)EXP

- ***necessities***:minors r bound on contracts for necessities(food,clothing,medical care,shelter).
- ***implied affirmation***:签合同时候未成年+后来成年+retains benefit of contract without complaint.

## 5.2 [M] duress

- physical compulsion[void]
- non-physical/economic duress[voidable by victim]

## 5.3 [M] undue influence [voidable by victim]

- ***unfair persuasion*** of a person[e.g.过度的压力]
- who is either:(a)***under the dominartion*** of the person exercising influence;
- in a ***sufficiently close relationship*** to justify believing that the other person will act in their interest or welfare.

## 5.4 [M]mistake[事实认识错误] & misunderstanding[条款理解不同]

### 5.4.1 mutual mistake  [共同误解]

> contract is voidable[受影响的一方可以撤销合同] if:

- ***both parties*** r mistaken;
- as to a ***basic assumption of fact***
- the mistake is ***material***(NOT price/value)
- the person asserting the mistake ***didn't bear the risk*** of the mistake.

### 5.4.2 unilateral mistake[单方面的事实认识错误]

> generally NOT a valid defense,occures when:

- a mistake by one party.
- that is unknown to the other party.
- concerning a basic assumption.
- has a material effect.

### 5.4.3 misunderstanding

#### (1)no contract formed

- contract term is ***material & ambiguous***.
- the parties have ***different subjective meanings*** of the contract term.
- neither/both parties know of misunderstanding.

#### (2)合同成立

- ***one party is aware of meaning***,合同按照对方所想成立
- if parties have same incorrect subjective meaning,a contract is formed according to that meaning.

## 5.5 [M]fraud,misrepresentation,& nondisclosure

### 5.5.1 fraudulent misrepresentation[仅受合同不利影响的一方可以撤销合同]

- D knowingly
- made a false representation,[欺诈的方式作出]
- of material fact,
- the other party reasonably relies on the misrepresentation to their detriment.[受不利影响的一方有理由依赖虚假陈述]

### 5.5.2 non-disclosure

> there is no duty to disclose UNLESS:

- active concealment[隐藏] occurs;
- a fiduciary relationship exists;
- it's necessary to correct an earlier mistake;
- failure to correct a past statement form being misleading.

### 5.5.3 concealment

> an ***affirmative act*** intended to keep another person from learning a fact.

- concealment is deemed a misrepresentation.

## 5.6 [M]illeqality

- ***illegal subject matter***:void
- ***illegal purpose***:voidable by the party who didn't know of the illegal purpose.
- ***subsequently became illegal***:performance is discharged.

## 5.7 [M]public policy

- ***restrictive covenant[限制性契约]*** is ENFORCEABLE if ***reasonable*** in (1)time,(2)geographic area,AND (3)scope of ***activity limited***.
- if found unreasonable ,MOST courts will enforce a restrictive convenant up to its ***reasonable limits***.

## 5.8 Unconscionability[显示公平]

- ***procedural unconscionability*** unfair bagaining.
- ***substantive unconscionability*** unfair terms.
- the court MAY:(a)refuse to enforce;(b)enforce the contract WITHOUT the unconscionable term;or (c)limit the application of the term.

## 5.9[H] SOF

### 5.9.1 writing requirement

- signed by the party to charged.[只要求被告签名,包括简签]
- reasonably identify the subject matter.
- indicate a contract was made
- state the essential terms.

### 5.9.2 contract subject SOF

- marriage
- year:从签订日期开始预计要超过一年才能完成的服务合同
- land,租约和地役权除外
- executor/administrator for promise：遗产管理人自掏腰包支付死者的债务
- goods for $500 or more:超过500美金的物品买卖合同
- suretyship[保证合同]：main purpose EXCP:NO writing is required if the main purpose was to benefit the surety.

### 5.9.3 UCC SOF EXCP

- ***merchant's confirmatory memorandum***,between 2 merchants,a writing signed only by the party enforcing it,and other party didn't promplty object within ***10 days***.[提出反对的书面形式必须有签名]
- ***goods accepted or paid for***,but only applies for those goods,not the whole contract.
- ***custom made goods***,seller made substantial start and the goods are not suitable for sale in the ordinary course of seller's business.
- ***judicial admission/acknowledgment***,an admission during a judicial proceeding.

### 5.9.4 CL SOF EXCP

- ***full performance***
- ***partial performance in land contracts***,need:(1)made payment for land,(2)took possession of land,(3)made valuable ***improvements*** to land.
- ***judicial acknowledgement***,party admits to the contract in pleadings or testimony.
- ***estoppel***,reasonable & foreseeable detrimental reliance(some jurisdictions).

# 6.contract content & meaning

## 6.1 [H] parol evidence rule[口头证据规则]

- 合同以书面为准,排除与书面合同相矛盾的证据;
- 不禁止关于双方当事人之间后续协议的证据（例如，双方在合同签订后就“额外项目”达成的口头修改）

### 6.1.1 EXP

- to correct a clerical error or typo.
- to establish a defense against formation.
- to interpret vague or ambiguous terms(but courts will interpret using the plain meaning rule).
- to supplement a partially integrated writing.

### 6.1.2 Partially integrated writing [不完备的合同]

> the writing doesn't contain a complete statement of all the terms.

- evidence is allowed if it doesn't contradict the writing.

### 6.1.3 fully integrated writing [完备的合同]

> a complete and exclusive statement of terms,discharges prior agreements.

- ***merger clause*** is evidence that the writing is complete on its face(fully integrated).

## 6.2 [M] UCC omitted & implied terms

> if not agreed upon,the court will fill in default contract terms(gap fillers).

### 6.2.1 UCC gap fillers

> 合同方、合同标的物、数量条款不适用[数量条款必须明确一个确定的数额，或者能够通过参照客观事实使其确定]。

- ***price***:reasonable price at time of delivery.
- ***time for payment***:due at time & place buyer is to receive goods.
- ***time for shipment/delivery***:within a reasonable time & at a reasonable hour.
- ***place for delivery***:(1)seller's place of  business if applicable;(2)seller's home;(3)some other place if parties know goods are there.

## 6.3 [M] UCC delivery & ROL

- ***if agreed upon***:contract terms govern ROL
- ***if breach***:ROL typically remains with breaching party.

### 6.3.1 shipment by common carrier

> ROL depends on whether it's a shipment or destination contract.

#### 1)shipment contract

- seller must deliver goods to a suitable carrier at ***seller's location***(FOB seller's location).
- ROL:passes to buyer when goods r delivered to the ***carrier***.

#### 2)destination contract

- seller must safely deliver goods to buyer's location[FOB buyer's location].
- ROL:passes to buyer when goods r delivered to the ***buyer***.

### 6.3.2 non-common carrier shipment

- ***merchant seller***:ROL passes to buyer upon ***receipt of goods***.
- ***non-merchant seller***:ROL passes to buyer upon ***tender of delivery***.

## 6.4 [M] UCC output/requirement contracts

- contract where the quantity is measured by the ***output of seller*** or ***requirement of buyer***.
- an unreasonably disproportionate increase in quantity is NOT allowed.

# 7.performance,breach & discharge

## [H] 7.1 material breach vs.minor breach(CL)

### 7.1.1 material breach/substantial performance doctrine

- occurs when a party DOESN'T render ***substantial performance***.
- a material breach ***excuses*** the non-breaching party's performance.

### 7.1.2 minor breach

- DOESN'T excuse performance.
- BUT,the non-breaching party may bring a separate action for damages.

### 7.1.3 to determine if the breach is material

- extent of performance;
- adequacy of compensation for loss to the non-breaching party;
- hardship;
- likelihood the breaching party will cure;
- whether the breach was intentional.

### 7.1.4 delayed performance

- a party's failure to perform by a specified time is typically NOT a material breach unless:
- 1)it ***significantly deprives the other party of the benefit***of the contract;
- 2)***time of essence clause***:a contract mandates completed performance by a specific date(requires ***time of essence***)

## 7.2 [M] UCC perfect tender rule & EXP

### 7.2.1 perfect tender rule

- seller must deliver conforming goods;
- the smallest non-conformity is a breach,and buyer may reject all or a portion of the goods.

### 7.2.2 EXP

- ***right to cure***:seller has a right to cure when:1)time for performance hasn't expired;2)if seller had reasonable grounds that substitue goods would be accepted.
- ***installment contract***:buyer can (1)***cancel contract***:ONLY IF an installment is so defective that it substantially impairs the value of the entire contract;(2)time to cure has passed.

## 7.3 [H] UCC acceptance & revocation of acceptance

### 7.3.1 acceptance of goods

- after reasonable opportunity to insepct,buyer ***signifies to seller that goods r conforming*** or that it will ***retain them despite a non-conformity***.
- ***fails to reject goods***:after a reasonable opportunity to inspect;
- does ***any act inconsistent with seller's ownership*** of the goods.
- buyer is obligated to pay for goods once accepted UNLESSS revocation of acceptance is allowed.

### 7.3.2 revocation of acceptance

- the non-conformity ***substantially impairs*** the value of the goods.
- either (a) the defect was ***difficult to discover***(a latent defect),(b)acceptance was reasonably induced by ***seller's assurances***,(c)the buyer had a ***reasonable assumption the defect would be cured.***

### 7.3.3 revocation of acceptance

- MUST occur within a ***reasonable time*** after the buyer discovers or should have discovered the nonconformity.
- not effective until buyer notifies the seller.
- must occur before there is any substantial change in the goods.

## 7.4 [M] obligation of good faith & fair dealing

- every contract contains an implied obligation of good faith and fair dealing to act ***honestly and fairly***>
- UCC requires:1)honesty in fact,and 2)observance of reasonable commercial standards of fair dealing.

## 7.5 [M] conditions

- makes performance contingent upon the occurence or completion of the condition.
- EXCUSE of conditions:1)***waiver***:voluntarily giving up protection:but can retract the waiver if the other party hasn't relied on it;2)***hinderance***:the protected party ***wrongfully prevents or fails to make a good faith effort*** to satisfy the condition.

## 7.6 [M]impossible,impracticability & frustration of purpose

### 7.6.1 impossibility

> performance is discharged when it's objectively impossible to perform,such as:

- ***death or incapacity*** of a person necessary to effectuate the contract;
- ***unanticipated destruction*** of the subject matter of the contract;
- ***supervening illegality*** a subsequent & unanticipated law/regulation makes performance illegal.

### 7.6.2 impracticablility

> performance is discharged when:

- an event occurs ***after contract formation***.
- that is ***unanticipated*** by both parties.
- making performance ***extremely and unreasonably difficult/expensive***.

### 7.6.3 frustration of purpose

> discharges performance when the ***purpose of the contract no longer exists***,occurs when:

- a party's principal purpose is substantially frustrated.
- by an unforeseeable superseding event outside their control.
- the event's non-occurence was a basic assumption of the contract.

## 7.7[H] anticipatory repudiation[预期违约]

### 7.7.1 non-breaching party may

> 对方明确表示即将违约,则受影响的一方可以：

- treat the contract as repudiated & sue for damages (before time of performance is due)[立刻起诉违约];
- treat the contract as discharged;[免除合同的履行责任]
- wait until performance is due and the sue[等履约期满起诉]
- urge the other party to perform.[敦促对方履行]

### 7.7.2 retact its repudiation[不安抗辩]

> a party can retract its repudiation and restore the contract UNLESS the non-breaching party has:

- cancelled the contract;
- materially changed its position;
- indicated that they consider the repudiation final.

### 7.7.3 insecurity & demand for adequate assurances

- 如果有理由相信对方无法履行合同，受影响的一方可以书面要求对方在合理时间内提供履约保证，如果不能按时提供保证，可以视为即将违约。
- 需要注意的是：1)必须有合理的原因，基于善意，相信对方无法履行合同 ;2)必须书面给出合理期限让对方提出担保，如果没有注明期限，UCC的规定是30天;3)不能提出明显不合理的担保要求。
